About Mouna African Hair Braiding

Crafting beautiful African hair styles with care and tradition for over a decade in the Chicagoland area.

Where Tradition Meets Modern Style

For over 10 years, Mouna African Hair Braiding & Weaves has been the trusted destination for flawless, long-lasting African hair styles in the Chicagoland area.

Whether you're looking for knotless braids, box braids, Senegalese twists, cornrows, or a custom look, our skilled team is here to bring your vision to life with precision, care, and creativity.

We believe braiding is more than a service - it's an art form rooted in culture and tradition. Our warm, professional team makes every visit feel like home. We don't just braid hair; we boost confidence and help you shine.

With two convenient locations in Bolingbrook and Joliet, expert African hair braiding is always close by. Our salon is a welcoming space where every client - adults and children alike - is treated with care and respect.
