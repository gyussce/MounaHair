Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

We recommend booking ahead to secure your preferred date and time. A $50 non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on availability, but appointments take priority.
Please arrive with your hair fully washed, detangled, and stretched (unless you've booked a wash or prep add-on). We offer washing and detangling for short hair at an additional $20. For Afro-textured or very tangled hair, we charge $40 for the wash service.
Yes, Kanekalon braiding hair is included with your service at no extra charge. Please note that human hair is not provided for boho knotless styles — however, we have human hair in all colors available for purchase separately in our salon. There is a $20 surcharge for color-mix requests.
Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid losing your deposit or being charged a cancellation fee. No-shows without prior communication may result in being blocked from future bookings.
We accept cash, Zelle, and Cash App. The $50 deposit is applied toward your total balance and must be paid at the time of booking. Tips are always appreciated but never required.
Yes! We welcome kids and offer discounts: $20 off for children 8 and under, and $30 off for children ages 8–12. We ask that children are cooperative and able to sit through the service. Please note that discounts only apply when hair is average length; long hair is subject to regular pricing.
We allow a 10–15 minute grace period. After that, we may need to reschedule or adjust the service depending on availability. We appreciate your punctuality so we can give you the full time and attention your style deserves.
We ask that you decide on your style before your appointment. Last-minute changes may not be accommodated depending on the complexity and time required for the new style.
Touch-up appointments should be booked within 2 weeks of your original style. Contact us to schedule your touch-up at either location.
Absolutely! We can accommodate two people at the same time. Please note there is an additional $50 charge for simultaneous appointments to ensure we have the staff available to serve you both.
