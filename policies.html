Important Info

Salon Policies

Please review our policies before booking your African hair braiding appointment.

Appointments & Deposits

  • Booking ahead is recommended for all services.
  • A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This amount goes toward your total balance and must be paid at the time of booking.
  • Appointments without a deposit will not be confirmed.

Cancellations & No-Shows

  • Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid losing your deposit or being charged a cancellation fee.
  • Missing your appointment without notice may result in being blocked from future bookings unless prior communication has been made.

Arrival & Preparation

  • A 10-15 minute grace period is allowed for late arrivals. After that, we may need to reschedule or adjust the service.
  • Arrive with clean, fully detangled, and stretched hair (unless you have booked a wash or prep add-on).
  • Washing and detangling service for short hair: $20 extra.
  • For Afro-textured or very tangled hair: $40 extra for washing service.

Style Changes & Touch-Ups

  • Please decide on your style before your appointment. Last-minute changes may not be accommodated.
  • Touch-up appointments should be booked within 2 weeks of your original style.

Kids' Services

  • We welcome kids but ask that they are cooperative and able to sit through the service.
  • $20 discount for children 8 and under.
  • $30 discount for children ages 8-12.
  • Discounts only apply when hair is average length. Long hair is subject to regular pricing.

Hair & Add-Ons

  • Kanekalon braiding hair is provided with your service.
  • No human hair is provided for boho knotless styles.
  • Human hair in all colors is available for purchase separately in our salon.
  • $20 extra for color-mix requests.
  • Two people booked simultaneously require a $50 additional charge.

Payment & Refunds

  • We accept cash, Zelle, and Cash App.
  • Tips are appreciated but never required.
  • Due to the nature of our services, no refunds are offered once the service is complete. If there is an issue, please let us know within 48 hours.
